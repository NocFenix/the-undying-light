Introduction

You are the crew of the USCSS Celerity. You regularly perform routine supply runs to the secretive Weyland-Yutani facility, Ferro Outpost, located in the Outer Rim Territories. Despite being a routine mission, it pays very well due to the aggressive climate of the small planet that Ferro Outpost is located on. Being the last stop in the supply run, part of the routine is to refuel the ship's planetfall capable shuttle The Missus, which tends to be low on fuel by this stage of the "Ferro Run."

The planet is far enough from its star that it is in a state of permafrost and always has freezing temperatures. It seems inhospitable, but this was part of the appeal when Weyland-Yutani built Ferro Outpost for top secret Research and Development; less prying eyes will be willing to face the cold so fierce that it can literally freeze exposed fuel lines.
